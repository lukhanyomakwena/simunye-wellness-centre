About Us!

Simunye Wellness Centre is a centre that is committed to delivering the highest quality of wellness services which include prevention and promotion of health for individuals and communities. Incorporated into the philosophy of the wellness centre is the integration of treating the whole person Mind, Body and Spirit.

Our Objectives!

  • Delivering the highest quality of wellness services which include prevention and promotion of health for all members of the community.
  • Render invaluable wellness services in an atmosphere of respect to the entire community.
  • Provide and promote innovative, culturally, and linguistically accessible wellness programs and service for the Khayelitsha community.
  • Provide programs that promote interventions aimed at addressing lifestyle including diet and exercise, offering an array of health and wellness classes.
  • Maintain prevailing knowledge of evolving wellness trends and respond with quality programs and education.
